Beach Clean Up

We Make the Beach Trash Free!

About Us

We make it our mission to fix this crime against nature. We start by cleaning up the average amount of plastic that an individual produces per year (105kg) each. Our goal is to be a reminder that each choice has a consequence and individual efforts add up.

Make it Happen!

Join our mission and take action at your local beach. We gather up at the East coast Lighthouse, do a warm up with some groovy tunes and start picking trash from the shore, moving southwest. The day is finished by weighting what we collected, after we move rewarding ourselves with hot (or cold) beverages, provided by our organization.
